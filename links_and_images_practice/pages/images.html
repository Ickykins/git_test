Images Practice

This page covers common image formats, but you may also be looking for our About page or our Homepage

JPG's

JPG images are good for photos with a lot of detail or color.

GIF's

GIF's are good for animations.

PNG's

PNGs are good for diagrams and icons. They have no limitations in color palette or transparancy.

SVGs

SVG images are vector based. This means they can scale as large or as small as needed without loosing quality.
